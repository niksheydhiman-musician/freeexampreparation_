## Why PYQ analysis matters

PYQ trends help you prioritize high-frequency themes and avoid random preparation.

## Recurring areas across exams

- Observation-based reasoning
- Visual logic and pattern interpretation
- Basic design principle application
- Creative storytelling through visuals
- Time-bound execution quality

## How to use this insight

- Divide topics into high, medium, and low frequency buckets.
- Allocate more revision loops to high-frequency concepts.
- Use PYQ-first strategy before advanced mock variation.
