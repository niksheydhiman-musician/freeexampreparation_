## Step 1: Take timed mocks seriously

Always simulate exam conditions: fixed time, no distractions, and no mid-test checking.

## Step 2: Analyze every attempt

After each mock, classify mistakes into:

- Concept gaps
- Misread questions
- Time management issues
- Careless errors

## Step 3: Plan the next week from errors

Your next week should be based on the error log—not random topic selection.

## Step 4: Re-test weak areas

Take focused mini-mocks on weak topics before the next full-length attempt.
