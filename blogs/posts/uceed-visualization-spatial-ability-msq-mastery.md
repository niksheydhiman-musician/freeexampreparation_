## Why MSQ feels difficult

MSQ requires both conceptual clarity and careful option checking. Even one wrong assumption can affect the entire response.

## What to practice daily

- 3D mental rotation drills
- Mirror/reflection and folding questions
- Pattern continuation and shape decomposition
- Perspective-based object reasoning

## Accuracy framework

1. Visualize first, then eliminate options.
2. Mark uncertain options and re-check at the end.
3. Avoid over-solving; focus on structure and constraints.

## Weekly improvement plan

- 3 timed micro-tests for spatial reasoning
- 1 full mixed UCEED set
- 1 review session with mistake categorization
