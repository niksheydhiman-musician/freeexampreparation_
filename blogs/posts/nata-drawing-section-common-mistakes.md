## Common mistakes candidates repeat

1. Poor composition planning
2. Weak perspective consistency
3. Unclear focal points
4. Over-shading without structure
5. Ignoring scale and proportion
6. Weak human/environment integration
7. Rushed finishing
8. Untidy annotations
9. No time buffer for correction
10. Not practicing under timed conditions

## Fix strategy

- Spend the first 2–3 minutes on layout planning.
- Use construction lines lightly.
- Keep shading purposeful and directional.
- Reserve final minutes only for refinement.

## Practice rule

Take at least two timed drawing simulations per week and review with a checklist.
