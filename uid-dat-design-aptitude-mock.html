Q27 Lateral Thinking • 10 Marks 📄 UID DAT 2024

🎯 Design Problem: ATM Cash Dispenser System

Problem Statement:

Identify a flaw in ATM system design where cash is dispensed BEFORE the card is returned. This creates a security and usability issue.

Task:

  • ✓ Identify the problem clearly
  • ✓ Propose a design solution with sketches
  • ✓ Write a concept statement (100 words max)
  • ✓ Show the improved user flow
💡 Solution Approach

Problem Analysis: Current sequence: Dispense Cash → Return Card creates risk of card abandonment and security threats.

Proposed Solution: Reverse the sequence: Eject Card FIRST → Dispense Cash ONLY after card is withdrawn.

Design Benefits: Improved safety, reduced card loss, clearer user priority (card > cash).

👗 Design Challenge: Regional Saree with Vernacular Typography

Design Brief:

Design a saree for a 40-year-old regional woman for a special event. Incorporate letters from the vernacular language of her region (max 10 letters) into the saree pattern.

Evaluation Criteria:

  • ✓ Typography integration in pattern

💡 Design Hints

Regional Options: Bengali, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam, or Hindi script integration

Design Approach: Weave vernacular letters into traditional patterns, creating modern-traditional fusion

Color Psychology: Warm, dignified tones suitable for mature elegance and special occasion wear
